Bundle Overview
===============

This bundle contains the Apollo 17 Lunar Ejecta And Meteorites (LEAM)
Experiment Calibration Notebook Collection that consists of a digital
reproduction of one hand-written laboratory notebook of calibration analysis
by Dr. Otto E. Berg, the principal investigator. Dr. Berg used this work
along with analyses of data acquired by the Pioneer 8 and 9 Cosmic Dust
Detectors, which were identical to LEAM detector, to calibrate the Apollo 17
LEAM data. The collection includes the LEAM chapter Apollo 17 Preliminary
Science Report as a supporting document.
